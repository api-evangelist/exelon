# Exelon (exelon)
Exelon Corporation is one of the largest energy delivery companies in the United States, serving more than 10 million customers through its regulated electric and gas utility subsidiaries: Atlantic City Electric, BGE, ComEd, Delmarva Power, PECO, and Pepco. Headquartered in Chicago, Illinois, Exelon operates across multiple U.S. states. This repository captures the APIs, developer tools, and machine-readable API artifacts for Exelon.
